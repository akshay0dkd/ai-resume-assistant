# 🚀 AI Resume Preparation Platform

An AI-powered career development platform that helps users optimize their resumes, evaluate ATS readiness, prepare for interviews, and generate personalized learning plans based on their skills, experience, and target roles.

## 📌 Overview

The AI Career Preparation Platform assists students, fresh graduates, and professionals in improving their job readiness through intelligent resume analysis and interview preparation tools.

The platform analyzes resumes, generates ATS scores, identifies strengths and weaknesses, creates personalized technical and behavioral interview questions, and recommends a structured preparation roadmap to improve employability.
